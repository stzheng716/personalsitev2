ABOUT ME

I’ve been working in commercial and residential for the past 6 years. During my time as a Project Manager at a real estate startup, I worked with internal stakeholders to build internal tools to increase job efficiency. That role allowed me to realize my passion for software engineering. I loved learned about the technical aspect of the project I was working on and always took extra time to engage with the engineers to learn more. In addition to working with engineers, I had the opportunity to build systems using Zapier, a low code automation tool for web applications. From there, I was hooked and knew I wanted to expand my skills to build complex and sophisticated applications by learning the fundamentals of coding.
